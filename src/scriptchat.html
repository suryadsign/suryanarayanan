Qualitative Research

Qualitative research involves in in-depth explorations and understanding of user experiences,behaviours and motivations. In the context of Scriptchat, This provides rich insights into the emotional and psychological aspects of conversator’s need and preference, helping to get sufficient insights that quantitative data alone may not capture.

User Interviews

  • Who do you communicate with most? What kind of messages do you send and receive?
  • How do you currently handle audio messages? Would written transcripts be helpful?
  • Do you feel overwhelmed by group messages? How do you stay on top of important conversations?
  • How would you use a private feed within a group? What kind of information would you share there?)
  • Do you have any accessibility concerns when using messaging apps? How would speech recognition improve your experience?
  • How comfortable are you with apps suggesting content? What kind of recommendations would be valuable to you?
  • Is there anything else related to these new features you'd like to share?
